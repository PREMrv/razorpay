Frequently asked questions

What is Razorpay Rize?

Razorpay Rize is a startup program focused on the growth journey of early stage and bootstrapped startups.
Who can join Razorpay Rize?

All bootstrapped and early stage startups who have raised no more than $5M in funding can join Razorpay Rize.
What are the benefits provided to startups under the program?
In the beta program, 3 benefits will be provided to startups -
  • Rize.Start - Incorporate your startup with us.
  • Rize.Tools - Curated set of Razorpay products, no-code tools and other tools with exclusive credits / discounts to accelerate your growth
  • Rize.Connect - Become part of a founders-only community
What is the process to join Razorpay Rize?
1. Now that you’re here, click on 'Join the Waitlist'
2. Enter details of your startup
3. Keep an eye out in your inbox. We will get right back!
Can startups who are already incorporated join Razorpay Rize?
Yes! You can still avail the benefits on Razorpay product, low code / no code tools, partner deals and become a part of the founders only community.
